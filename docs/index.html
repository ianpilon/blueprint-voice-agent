        The method
        
How it turns talk into a spec

      

      
You speak in plain language. The agent works through seven lenses, at your pace, and quietly assembles a buildable specification underneath the conversation.

      

        

          1
          

            
Vision — What it is

            
A one-sentence, plain-language description of the thing you want to build, and why

          

          Lens
        

        

          2
          

            
Users — Who it's for

            
The real person who reaches for it, and the moment they open it

          

          Lens
        

        

          3
          

            
Core job — The one thing it must do

            
The single action that, if it didn't work, the whole app would be pointless

          

          Lens
        

        

          4
          

            
The flow — Step by step

            
A walkthrough of the core action, where the real screens and features fall out

          

          Lens
        

        

          5
          

            
Memory — What it keeps track of

            
The information the app stores, which becomes the data model behind it

          

          Lens
        

        

          6
          

            
Look & feel — How it comes across

            
Phone or web, simple or rich, and any screen you can already picture

          

          Lens
        

        

          7
          

            
Boundaries — Where version one stops

            
The smallest useful first version, and what's deliberately left for later

          

          Lens
        

        

          8
          

            
Your build spec — Read back and locked

            
The whole thing played back in build order: overview, users, flow, data, and version-one scope
